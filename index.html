Social Hook runs your marketing while you run your code. It watches what you build, drafts what matters, and publishes where your audience lives. Free for Claude Code subscribers.

The Pipeline

One commit. Many voices.

Each platform gets its own identity. The evaluator reads your work, decides what matters, and drafts content shaped to each audience. Commits chain into narratives. One push becomes a thread on X, a deep dive on LinkedIn, a visual on Instagram.

Differentiator

Built for agents, not just humans

A coding agent builds your product and hands off to Social Hook. Marketing runs autonomously. No context-switching. No copy-paste. The developer never leaves the terminal.

Every other tool assumes a human is at the controls. Social Hook doesn't.
